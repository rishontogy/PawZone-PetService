PAWZONE – LIVE LOCATION & VIDEO UPLOAD DATE RESTRICTIONS

CRITICAL REQUIREMENT:

The transporter's Live Location, Pickup Video, and Delivery Video must ONLY be available on the actual transport day.

They must not be accessible before or after the assigned transport date.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
📅 1. DATE CALCULATION VALIDATION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

First ensure automatic route date calculation is working correctly.

When transporter accepts an order:

System must calculate:

Assigned Route Date

based on:

✔ Transporter's selected route day
✔ Current date
✔ 7:00 AM cutoff rule

Then automatically save:

pickupDate
dropDate

to the order.

These dates become the master transport dates.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
📍 2. LIVE LOCATION VISIBILITY RULE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Live Location must ONLY be visible when:

Current Date = Pickup Date

AND

Order Status = Picked Up / In Transit

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
BEFORE PICKUP DATE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Buyer:
❌ Cannot view live location

Seller:
❌ Cannot view live location

Show:

"Live tracking will be available on the pickup date."

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
ON PICKUP DATE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Buyer:
✔ Can view tracking

Seller:
✔ Can view tracking

Admin:
✔ Can always view tracking

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
AFTER DELIVERY COMPLETED
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Buyer:
❌ Tracking hidden

Seller:
❌ Tracking hidden

Show:

"Tracking session completed."

Admin:
✔ Historical record remains available

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
🎥 3. PICKUP VIDEO RESTRICTION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Transporter Pickup Video upload must ONLY be enabled when:

Current Date = Pickup Date

Before Pickup Date:

❌ Upload disabled

After Pickup Date:

❌ Upload disabled

Only on Pickup Date:

✔ Upload enabled

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
🚚 4. PICKUP VIDEO WORKFLOW
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

On Pickup Date:

Transporter uploads Pickup Video

System automatically:

✔ Marks order as Picked Up
✔ Changes status to In Transit
✔ Creates timeline entry
✔ Sends notifications

Buyer:
"Pet has been picked up."

Seller:
"Pet has been picked up."

Admin:
Alert generated.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
🎥 5. DELIVERY VIDEO RESTRICTION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Delivery Video upload must ONLY be enabled when:

Current Date = Drop Date

Before Drop Date:

❌ Upload disabled

After Drop Date:

❌ Upload disabled

Only on Drop Date:

✔ Upload enabled

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
📦 6. DELIVERY VIDEO WORKFLOW
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Transporter uploads Delivery Video

System automatically:

✔ Marks order as Delivered
✔ Creates delivery record
✔ Sends notification

Buyer:
"Delivery completed. Please confirm delivery."

Seller:
"Delivery completed."

Admin:
Alert generated.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
⏳ 7. MISSED DATE PROTECTION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

If transporter misses upload date:

Show:

"Pickup date missed."

or

"Delivery date missed."

Create Admin Alert:

Transporter failed to upload required transport proof.

Admin can:

✔ Extend date
✔ Reassign transporter
✔ Contact transporter

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
👨‍💼 8. ADMIN OVERRIDE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Admin may override restrictions.

Admin can:

✔ View all videos
✔ View tracking
✔ Reopen upload window
✔ Extend transport dates

This is for dispute resolution.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
🔒 9. SECURITY RULES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Live Location visible ONLY to:

✔ Assigned Buyer
✔ Assigned Seller
✔ Assigned Transporter
✔ Admin

Never visible to:

❌ Other Buyers
❌ Other Sellers
❌ Other Transporters

🎯 FINAL REQUIREMENT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Live tracking and transport proof uploads must be controlled entirely by the automatically calculated pickup/drop dates.

Buyers and sellers should only see tracking on the actual transport day, and transporters should only be able to upload pickup or delivery videos on their respective assigned dates.
