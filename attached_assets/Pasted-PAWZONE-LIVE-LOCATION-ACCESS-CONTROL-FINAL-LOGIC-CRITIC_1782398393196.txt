The transporter's live location must NOT be visible immediately after upload.

It should become available ONLY when ALL required conditions are satisfied.

paymentStatus = COMPLETED

today = pickupDate

today = dropDate

orderStatus ≠ DELIVERED

✔ Close tracking session

Seller:
