Update the product listing and inventory system to handle stock depletion and visibility correctly.

This is a CRITICAL marketplace behavior update.

---

# 🧠 1. CORE REQUIREMENT

---

When a product’s stock reaches **0**:

→ It must be **hidden from buyer view (marketplace)**
→ But NOT deleted from the system

---

# ❌ CURRENT PROBLEM

---

* Products still visible even when out of stock
* Buyers try to purchase unavailable pets
* Poor user experience

---

# ✅ 2. REQUIRED BEHAVIOR

---

## 📦 When Order Completes:

* Reduce product quantity

---

## 🔻 If Quantity = 0:

---

### Buyer Side:

* REMOVE product card from:

  * Dashboard
  * Category pages
  * Search results

---

### ❗ DO NOT DELETE PRODUCT

---

## 🗂 3. DATA RETENTION (IMPORTANT)

---

Even after stock = 0:

Product must still exist in database for:

---

### Admin:

* Full order history
* Financial tracking
* Audit logs

---

### Seller:

* Listing history
* Performance analytics

---

# 🔄 4. RESTOCK LOGIC

---

## When seller adds stock again:

---

### Condition:

```id="stock01"
IF quantity > 0:
    product.is_active = true
```

---

## Result:

* Product becomes visible again to buyers
* Reappears in:

  * Dashboard
  * Categories
  * Search

---

# ⚙️ 5. IMPLEMENTATION RULE

---

## Add a field in product database:

```id="stock02"
is_active (boolean)
```

---

## Logic:

```id="stock03"
IF quantity <= 0:
    is_active = false
ELSE:
    is_active = true
```

---

## Buyer queries must ONLY fetch:

```id="stock04"
WHERE is_active = true
```

---

# 🧪 6. VALIDATION CASES

---

## Case 1:

* Product quantity = 5
  ✔ Visible

---

## Case 2:

* Product purchased → quantity = 0
  ✔ Disappears from buyer view

---

## Case 3:

* Seller restocks → quantity = 3
  ✔ Product reappears

---

## Case 4:

* Admin checks product
  ✔ Still exists in system

---

# ⚠️ 7. IMPORTANT EDGE CASE

---

## Multi-seller scenario:

If same pet breed exists from multiple sellers:

* Only THAT seller’s card disappears
* Others remain visible

---

# 🎯 8. UI BEHAVIOR

---

## DO NOT show:

* “Out of stock” cards

---

## Instead:

* Completely hide them from buyers

---

# 🔐 9. ADMIN VISIBILITY

---

Admin must still see:

* All products (including out of stock)
* With status:

  * Active
  * Out of Stock

---

# 📊 10. SELLER DASHBOARD

---

Seller must see:

* Their products (including out of stock)
* Option to:

  * Restock
  * Edit

---

# 🎯 FINAL REQUIREMENT

---

After this update:

✔ Buyers ONLY see available pets
✔ No out-of-stock confusion
✔ Products not deleted
✔ Admin retains full records
✔ Seller can restock anytime

---

## FINAL RULE:

Inventory visibility ≠ Data deletion

Products must be hidden, not removed.
