PAWZONE – MANDATORY TERMS & CONDITIONS ACCEPTANCE DURING SIGN UP

Implement a mandatory Terms & Conditions acceptance flow for all three user types:
• Buyer
• Seller
• Transporter

Do NOT change any existing signup validations. Only add the Terms & Conditions flow.

----------------------------------------------------
1. Terms & Conditions Checkbox
----------------------------------------------------

At the bottom of every signup form, add:

☐ I have read and agree to the Terms & Conditions.

The checkbox must be DISABLED initially.

Users cannot click it immediately.

----------------------------------------------------
2. View Terms Button
----------------------------------------------------

Above the checkbox show:

View Terms & Conditions

This should be a clickable button/link.

When clicked:

Open a full-screen modal (or separate page) displaying the correct Terms & Conditions document based on the selected account type.

Buyer → Buyer Terms & Conditions

Seller → Seller Terms & Conditions

Transporter → Transporter Terms & Conditions

Do NOT show the wrong document.

----------------------------------------------------
3. Enable Checkbox
----------------------------------------------------

The checkbox should become enabled ONLY AFTER the user has opened the Terms & Conditions page.

Simply opening the document is sufficient.

After the document has been opened:

✔ Enable the checkbox.

Before opening it:

Checkbox remains disabled.

----------------------------------------------------
4. Signup Validation
----------------------------------------------------

The Sign Up button must not work unless:

• All existing validations pass.
• Terms document has been opened.
• Checkbox is checked.

Otherwise show:

"Please read and accept the Terms & Conditions before creating your account."

----------------------------------------------------
5. Remember During Current Signup
----------------------------------------------------

If the user opens the Terms page once during the current signup process:

Keep the checkbox enabled.

If they close the Terms page:

Do NOT disable it again.

Only reset if the signup form itself is reset or the page is refreshed.

----------------------------------------------------
6. Terms Viewer
----------------------------------------------------

The Terms page should have:

• Proper title.
• Scrollable content.
• Nice readable formatting.
• Close button.
• Back button.

Do NOT automatically check the checkbox.

The user must manually tick it.

----------------------------------------------------
7. Responsive
----------------------------------------------------

Must work perfectly on:

• Desktop
• Tablet
• Mobile

----------------------------------------------------
8. Security
----------------------------------------------------

Never allow account creation if:

✔ Terms were never opened.

OR

✔ Checkbox is not checked.

Both conditions are mandatory.

----------------------------------------------------
9. Existing Functionality
----------------------------------------------------

Do NOT modify:

• Login
• Registration logic
• Backend authentication
• Approval workflow
• Admin verification

Only add the Terms & Conditions acceptance flow.
