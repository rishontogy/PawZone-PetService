# 🛒 3. CART AUTO-RESTORE SYSTEM (INVENTORY LOCK FIX)

---

## ❌ Problem:

* Items added to cart are locked indefinitely
* Blocks inventory

---

## ✅ Required Behavior:

---

## ⏱ Cart Lock Duration:

* **3 Hours**

---

## 🔹 Logic:

---

### When item added to cart:

```js
cart_item.lock_time = current_time
cart_item.expiry_time = current_time + 3 hours
```

---

---

### Background Job:

Run check periodically:

```js
IF current_time > expiry_time AND order_not_placed:
    REMOVE item from cart
    RESTORE product quantity
```

---

---

## 🔁 Restore Behavior:

* Product quantity increases back
* Product becomes visible again if needed
