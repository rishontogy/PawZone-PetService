PAWZONE – KCI CERTIFICATION FEATURE FOR DOGS & CATS

REQUIREMENT:

Add a new KCI Certification section in the Seller → Add Listing page.

This section should only appear for:

✔ Dogs
✔ Cats

Do NOT show this section for:

❌ Birds
❌ Fish
❌ Rabbits
❌ Hamsters
❌ Guinea Pigs
❌ Turtles
❌ Exotic Pets
❌ Any other animal categories

FINAL REQUIREMENT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

For Dog and Cat listings only:

• Add "KCI Certified" toggle.
• If enabled, certificate upload becomes mandatory.
• Store certificate in database.
• Show certificate in Admin approval page.
• Show KCI badge and certificate viewer in Buyer pet details page.
• Hide entire KCI section for all non-dog and non-cat animals.
