🚚 1. AUTO PICKUP & DROP DATE LOGIC
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Current transporter acceptance flow is incomplete.

The system must automatically determine the Pickup Date and Drop Date from the transporter's route schedule.

No manual date entry required.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
✅ REQUIRED LOGIC
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

When a transporter accepts an order:

Step 1:
Check today's date.

Step 2:
Check the route day selected by the transporter.

Step 3:
Calculate the next occurrence of that route day.

Step 4:
Automatically assign:

Pickup Date = Route Date
Drop Date = Route Date

⏰ 2. 7:00 AM ROUTE CUTOFF RULE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

For same-day routes:

If current time is BEFORE 7:00 AM:

✔ Transporter can Accept
✔ Transporter can Reject

If current time is AFTER 7:00 AM:

❌ Same-day route cannot be accepted

System must automatically move to the next occurrence of that route day.
