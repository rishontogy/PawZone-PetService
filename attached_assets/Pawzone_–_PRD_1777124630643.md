# **Pawzone – Live Pet Selling Platform PRD**

### **TL;DR**

Pawzone is a Kerala-based mobile and web platform connecting verified pet sellers—including breeders, pet shops, and individual owners—with buyers seeking healthy, ethically-sourced pets. Prioritizing trust, animal welfare transparency, and a seamless discovery-to-purchase journey, Pawzone aims to be Kerala’s \#1 live pet marketplace and a trusted bridge between local pet lovers and responsible sellers.

---

## **Goals**

### **Business Goals**

* Achieve market leader status as the \#1 live pet marketplace in Kerala within 12 months.  
* Onboard 200+ verified pet sellers during Phase 1 of launch.  
* Facilitate at least 500 successful live pet transactions in the first 6 months.  
* Generate sustainable revenue through seller listing fees and paid featured placements.  
* Build strong brand credibility rooted in animal welfare and trust.

### **User Goals**

* For buyers: discover and purchase healthy, verified pets nearby with clear details on breed and health.  
* For sellers: simple, streamlined process to list pets and reach a large, engaged local audience.  
* Both: confidence in a safe, fraud-resistant, and transparent transaction environment.  
* Platform-wide: access to ratings/reviews and quick, reliable communication.  
* Assurance of animal welfare compliance and clear documentation (health certificates).

### **Non-Goals**

* No international shipping, or listing/sale of exotic or illegal wildlife.  
* No integrated veterinary consultation services in Phase 1\.  
* No pet food, accessories, or non-live animal products in MVP/Phase 1\.

## **User Stories**

### **Pet Buyer (Individual)**

* As a pet buyer, I want to search pets by location, breed, price, and age so that I can quickly find pets available nearby.  
* As a pet buyer, I want to view health certificates and clear photos so that I’m confident the pet is healthy and authentic.  
* As a pet buyer, I want to save listings as favorites so that I can compare options or revisit later.  
* As a pet buyer, I want to contact the seller via in-app chat so that I can negotiate or clarify details safely.  
* As a pet buyer, I want to pay a booking deposit online to reserve a pet before pickup.

### **Pet Seller/Breeder (Individual or Shop)**

* As a seller, I want to create and manage listings quickly, uploading all required details and images, so that my pets are visible to buyers.  
* As a seller, I want to receive real-time inquiries and notifications so that I never miss a potential sale.  
* As a seller, I want my listings to appear in featured sections via paid promotion to boost my sales.  
* As a seller, I want to manage inquiries, mark pets as sold, and see analytics on my dashboard.

### **Platform Admin**

* As an admin, I want to verify seller IDs, licenses, and shop proofs so that only responsible and legal sellers are listed.  
* As an admin, I want to moderate listings and handle reports to maintain platform quality and compliance.  
* As an admin, I want access to analytics dashboards and the ability to resolve disputes and monitor transactions.

## **Functional Requirements**

* **Pet Listings** (Priority: High)  
  * Create listing with breed, age, health certificate upload, price, multiple photos, and location.  
  * Search and filter listings by species, breed, location (Kerala districts/towns), price range, and age.  
  * Mark listing as sold/available.  
* **User Accounts** (Priority: High)  
  * Buyer and seller registration with mobile OTP verification.  
  * Seller verification: upload ID, shop proof, relevant animal welfare permits.  
  * Profile pages: seller ratings, reviews, and total pets sold.  
* **Communication** (Priority: High)  
  * In-app chat between buyers and sellers.  
  * Inquiry notifications via app and optional WhatsApp.  
* **Trust & Safety** (Priority: High)  
  * Seller badge system based on verification and ratings.  
  * Health certificate/document upload required for listings.  
  * Feature to report fraud, suspicious listings, or animal welfare issues.  
* **Payments** (Priority: Medium)  
  * Secure booking deposit option via UPI/online payment; payment holds pet until pickup.  
* **Featured Listings** (Priority: Medium)  
  * Sellers can pay to promote listings to top/banners for increased visibility (with clear “featured” badge).  
* **Admin Panel** (Priority: Medium)  
  * Moderation tools for listings and user reports.  
  * Seller verification and approval dashboard.  
  * Platform analytics (active listings, response times, issue tracking).

## **User Experience**

**Entry Point & First-Time User Experience**

* Users discover Pawzone via app download (Play Store) or web platform.  
* On launch: Role selection screen (Buyer/Seller), streamlined onboarding.  
* Registration via mobile number and OTP verification; location set via GPS or manual input (limited to Kerala).  
* For sellers: guided seller verification with document upload; buyers skip verification but must complete profile.

**Core Experience**

*Pet Buyer Flow*

* **Step 1:** Homepage shows feed of nearby and trending pet listings.  
  * Clean card layouts with breed, photos, location, price.  
  * Use of geolocation to prioritize relevant listings.  
* **Step 2:** User filters/searches by breed, species (dog, cat, bird, etc.), price, or location.  
  * Responsive filters for fast, intuitive narrowing of options.  
  * Zero-results experience suggests removing filters or searching nearby areas.  
* **Step 3:** User taps into a pet listing detail page.  
  * Multiple photos, breed details, health certificate (PDF/image), seller profile (with verification badge, reviews), price, and age.  
  * “Contact Seller” in-app chat button present, with option to save listing as favorite.  
* **Step 4:** Initiate chat with seller (app, push, optional WhatsApp notification to seller).  
  * Conversation history saved; system prompts for booking deposit if seller enables.  
* **Step 5:** (Optional) Pay booking deposit via UPI to hold pet (clear refund/cancellation policies shown).  
  * Automated notifications on successful deposit and reservation timestamp.  
* **Step 6:** Offline or coordinated pet pickup, user marks pet as received.

*Pet Seller Flow*

* **Step 1:** Register and complete seller verification (upload documents, enter shop/KYC details).  
  * Instant feedback on verification status; in-app tips to speed approval.  
* **Step 2:** Create new pet listing.  
  * Upload photos (up to 6 images), fill breed/age/health/price/location fields.  
  * Require health certificate upload.  
  * Option to promote as a featured listing (paid).  
* **Step 3:** Dashboard lists current, sold, and pending listings, with inquiries and analytics.  
  * Push notifications and dashboard inbox for new pupil inquiries.  
* **Step 4:** Mark pets as sold, respond to reviews, track deposit payments.

**Advanced Features & Edge Cases**

* Saved searches for buyers, with push notifications for new matching listings.  
* Seller analytics dashboard: views, response time, sales conversion.  
* Graceful handling of failed OTP (retry/resend flow); support for slow connections/low-bandwidth.  
* Error messaging and guidance for failed uploads or unverified seller actions.  
* Reporting/flagging listings for animal welfare/fraud (escalates to admin queue).

**UI/UX Highlights**

* Large, clear pet photos with priority given to image quality.  
* High-contrast, readable design tailored for mobile-first users in Kerala.  
* Simple navigation; all critical CTAs (Contact, Save, Report) are one-tap.  
* Multi-language support (English, Malayalam) prioritized for local relevance.  
* Accessibility: font size scaling, tap targets, colorblind-friendly palette.

## **Narrative**

Arun, a first-time pet buyer from Kochi, has always dreamt of adopting a Labrador puppy. His search on generic classifieds leaves him frustrated—details are sparse, photos are unclear, and trust is low. Then he tries Pawzone. Instantly, Arun filters listings by breed, age, and Thiruvananthapuram proximity. He spots a well-documented Labrador listing, complete with multiple high-quality photos and a visible health certificate. The seller’s profile is verified and features glowing reviews.

Arun uses Pawzone’s in-app chat to discuss details and, feeling reassured, pays a small deposit to reserve the puppy for pick-up the following weekend. The process is clear and secure, with automated updates and clear policies. The breeder, meanwhile, is thrilled—on generic platforms, he rarely receives qualified leads, but just in the first week on Pawzone, genuine inquiries surge. Arun brings the puppy home with confidence, and both buyer and seller submit glowing reviews. Pawzone has succeeded as the trusted, transparent, and welfare-conscious companion for Kerala’s pet community.

## **Technical Considerations**

### **Technical Needs**

* Android-first mobile application; responsive web version for broader access.  
* Robust backend API to support listings, chats, payments, verification.  
* Secure image storage for pet photos and document uploads.  
* GPS/geolocation utility for listing and browsing by Kerala districts.  
* Push notification system for critical updates, inquiries, and saved search triggers.  
* OTP-based authentication for new user/account creation.

### **Integration Points**

* UPI payment gateway for booking deposits.  
* WhatsApp Business API for optional seller inquiry alerts.  
* Google Maps integration for accurate, user-friendly location input and display.

### **Data Storage & Privacy**

* Secure storage of PII and sensitive documents; comply strictly with Indian IT Act and data localization rules.  
* No display of seller contact info until buyer initiates verified contact.  
* Data minimization and access control for admin functions.  
* Encrypted chat history and transaction data.

### **Scalability & Performance**

* Designed to serve up to 10,000 concurrent users in Phase 1, with architecture extensible to pan-India demand.  
* Efficient image and document delivery; content caching for fast load times.  
* Monitoring for capacity and quick remediation of performance bottlenecks.

### **Potential Challenges**

* Enforcing seller documentation and animal welfare compliance (manual \+ automated checks).  
* Preventing fraudulent or duplicate listings (smart moderation, admin review, user reporting).  
* Maintaining photo/document upload speed and quality on variable mobile networks.  
* Handling rapid scaling as platform gains traction statewide.
