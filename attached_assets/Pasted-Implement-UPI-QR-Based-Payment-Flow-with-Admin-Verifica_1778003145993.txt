# 📤 4. SUBMIT FLOW

---

## On clicking “Done”:

```js id="submit_payment"
order.payment_status = "PENDING_VERIFICATION";

savePaymentProof({
  order_id: order.id,
  screenshot: image,
  reference,
  date,
  status: "PENDING"
});
```

# ❌ 8. REJECT FLOW

---

## First rejection:

```js id="reject_once"
order.payment_status = "RETRY_ALLOWED";
notifyBuyer("Payment rejected. Please upload again.");
```

---

---

## Second rejection:

```js id="reject_twice"
order.payment_status = "FAILED";
order.status = "CANCELLED";

restockItems(order);
notifyBuyer("Order cancelled due to invalid payment");
```
